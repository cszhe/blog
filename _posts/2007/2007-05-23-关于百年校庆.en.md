May 20, 2007 was Tongji University's centennial anniversary. After it passed, I suddenly felt it had nothing to do with me.

Main events:

May Flowers performance in Jiading — weekend, I wasn't on Jiading campus. When I came back, the flowers had already bloomed.

May 20 daytime celebration — no ticket.

May 20 evening "Eternal Youth" gala — no ticket either.

May 20 alumni return — waited at Jiading campus for ages, no alumni showed up... crying.

The only thing related to me was the Party's official newspaper, People's Daily, front page, top story:
