I can't call myself a true Harry Potter fan. Last summer, when I was helpless in Zhangjiang, to keep my English from going rusty, I bought the English versions of Harry Potter 1-6 at a very low price from a street stall (pirated, of course). I read them all in one summer. After all, they're books for kids, not hard. I didn't learn much beautiful vocabulary, but I did learn things like wands and spells.

On July 21, the final Harry Potter book was released globally. I pre-ordered it a month early at the school bookstore. Cost me 208 RMB. I got the hardcover English version at the earliest opportunity. After buying so many pirated copies, at least I contributed once to the real thing.

As for the ending, it's basically a perfect happy ending. Nothing surprising. I just wonder when I'll finish reading it...
