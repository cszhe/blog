Today I went on a business trip to Changzhou to do training at Shinco. When I came back and opened my Gmail, I was surprised to find an email from the Beijing Olympic Ticketing Center. The Olympic tickets I applied for online long ago were successful! I got 4 tickets total — basketball and badminton. Basketball is the sport I love; badminton is what she loves. I know nothing about badminton. Basketball — haha — I'll closely follow the Olympic basketball schedule, hoping the game I bought tickets for has the Dream Team or the Chinese team. That would be perfect.

I had given up hope on the application, but I won the lottery. Guess my RP is really good. The tickets probably have the potential to appreciate 10-fold. The only downside is that neither game is at the Bird's Nest or Water Cube. But being able to watch the Olympics in Beijing in 2008 — that alone makes life complete.
