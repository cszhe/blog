With the project manager chasing you from the front and the customer hunting you from behind, who the hell would draw UML diagrams? — Chen Guofeng, Class 00 SE, on UML application in projects

Back in the day, every OS authority had one, and I had one too. — Professor Chen Hongzhong, OS instructor, on "Lion's Commentary on UNIX Source Code"

If you touch Security, you won't even know how you die. — Cheng Juntao, Class 00 SE, on the complexity of .NET security structure
