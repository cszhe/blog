Competition Introduction:

Windows CE is Microsoft's modular real-time embedded OS for mobile intelligent connected devices. It's widely used in PDAs, smartphones, automotive electronics, and information terminals.

Huaheng Technology (http://www.hhcn.com) is a renowned domestic embedded Linux and Windows CE platform provider. Tongji Software Institute and Huaheng jointly organize this "Huaheng Cup" Windows CE Development Competition to:
1. Prepare for the Microsoft-Tongji Windows Embedded Teaching Symposium in Spring 2005
2. Promote embedded systems and Windows CE knowledge
3. Provide a stage for developers and outstanding works
4. Cultivate innovation awareness

**Eligibility:** All Tongji undergraduate, master's, and PhD students.

**Registration:** Dec 15, 2004 – Feb 1, 2005. Email or phone.

**Submission Deadline:** Feb 20, 2005

**Awards:** First prize (3 teams, 3000 RMB each), Second prize (6 teams, 2000 RMB each), Third prize (12 teams, 1000 RMB each).
