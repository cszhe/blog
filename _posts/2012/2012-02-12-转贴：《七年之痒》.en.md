I rarely repost anything. In seven or eight years of blogging, I've only reposted one article before. Today, while browsing online, I came across a post by a fellow Tongji colleague and felt a deep resonance. So I'm reposting it.

Same school, same engineering background. To borrow a phrase I learned today: engineers have the advantage of being down-to-earth, and the disadvantage of being too down-to-earth. This guy exemplifies it perfectly.

No matter what seed you plant in this soil, it can only produce this kind of flower and this kind of fruit. That's the system.
