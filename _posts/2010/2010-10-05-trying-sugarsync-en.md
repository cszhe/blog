After years of working, I've accumulated several GB of documents that need regular backup. Since cloud storage emerged, I saw hope for backups. Dropbox was great, but then it got blocked. As the saying goes, "Not even wildfire can destroy it; the spring breeze brings it back to life." A new thing called SugarSync appeared. I tried it and found it even better than Dropbox in some ways. Registration gives 2GB for free. Supports Windows and Mac. Spent one evening uploading 2.1GB of stuff from my machine, then downloaded it to another laptop. Tried modifying and syncing. Works great.
