Woke up in the morning, saw my wife getting up for work. Glanced at my iPad on the nightstand: 5:20. Went straight back to sleep, thinking "Wow, honey's really motivated today."

Zzzz&#8230; for what felt like a long time. But something felt off. Why am I sleeping so well? Got up to check. The wall clock said 10am. My iPad showed 7am. A full three hours slow. Damn. No need to go to work today.

Searched online. Found forums in mainland China, Taiwan, and the US all filled with complaints. Apple's official forum had 6 pages of discussion with no resolution. Seems like a widespread issue.
