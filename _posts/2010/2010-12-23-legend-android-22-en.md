Today I accidentally clicked "check for upgrade" on my phone. A dialog popped up saying 2.2 is available. What a pleasant surprise. My Indian version HTC Legend, jokingly called the "third edition" online, actually got an update.

Immediately started the upgrade. After half an hour of waiting, success! Haven't noticed major changes so far. Gmail and Google Maps UI changed a lot. Can now move apps to SD card &#8212; the official app2sd. Good news for a small-storage device like the Legend. My Legend was already full of apps. There's also the claimed performance improvement, but I can't really feel it in actual use.
