With the strong support of classmates from the Tongji University Software School Windows CE Club, and the assistance of Microsoft's Ma Dan, this book has finally been published after a year of effort!! This is the first book I have participated in translating.

So far, I have three books authored or translated, still far from "towering with works" (it seems Wu Dalang has the easiest time achieving this — being short has its advantages in this regard). They are:

1. "Windows CE Embedded System" — First independently written technical book

[http://www.china-pub.com/32406](http://www.china-pub.com/32406 "http://www.china-pub.com/32406")

2. "Introduction to Embedded Software Development" — A basic book written to fulfill the Ministry of Education's Eleventh Five-Year textbook project. Simple content.

<http://www.china-pub.com/44881>

3. "Windows CE 6.0 Developer Reference" — A translated book. This one is quite expensive:

<http://www.china-pub.com/195314>
