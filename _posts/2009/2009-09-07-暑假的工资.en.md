Everyone thinks teachers can relax and have fun during summer vacation. Sigh, only we know the truth.

Since I started working, I haven't had a single summer off. Summer 2007 I was working on an aerospace project. Summer 2008 I was sent to Dingxi, Gansu. Summer 2009 I was applying for academic programs...

Summer vacation is really teachers' unpaid labor month. We don't do any less work, but the compensation is as shown below:

[<img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10273" height="172" src="/uploads/2009/09/SummerSalary-300x172.png" title="SummerSalary" width="300" />](/uploads/2009/09/SummerSalary.png)

We might as well just cancel winter and summer breaks. At least people wouldn't think all teachers are having a great time during breaks.
