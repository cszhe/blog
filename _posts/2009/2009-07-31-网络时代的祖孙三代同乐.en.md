I just grabbed a screenshot, but it captures what I've been doing every evening for months.

The baby is in Weihai escaping the summer heat, with his grandparents watching him. I'm working in Shanghai. Only through the internet can I see the baby's smiles and laughter 😀

Grandpa plays with the baby: Grandpa, Dad, and Baby all laugh heartily. The internet bridges the distance between people thousands of miles apart. Truly, a single "line" connects those far apart (actually I use wireless internet, not even a network cable...).
